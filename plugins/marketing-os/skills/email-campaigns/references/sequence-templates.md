# Email Sequence Templates

Blueprint structures for common email sequences. Customize timing, copy, and branching for each brand.

## Welcome / Onboarding Sequence

**Trigger:** New subscriber or account signup
**Goal:** Build relationship, deliver first value, set expectations
**Duration:** 7-14 days, 5-7 emails

| Email | Timing | Purpose | Subject Pattern |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Immediate | Welcome + quick win | "Welcome to [Brand] — here's your [promised resource]" |
| 2 | Day 2 | Brand story + values | "Why we started [Brand]" |
| 3 | Day 4 | Best content / resource | "Our most popular [resource type]" |
| 4 | Day 7 | Social proof | "What [X] customers say about [Brand]" |
| 5 | Day 10 | Advanced tip or guide | "The [topic] mistake most people make" |
| 6 | Day 12 | Soft sell / CTA | "Ready to [desired action]?" |
| 7 | Day 14 | Feedback request | "Quick question about your experience" |

**Branch at Email 4:** If clicked -> fast-track to Email 6 (soft sell). If no opens on emails 2-4 -> re-engagement variant.

## Lead Nurture Sequence

**Trigger:** Lead magnet download or demo request
**Goal:** Educate, build trust, convert to customer
**Duration:** 3-6 weeks, 6-10 emails

| Email | Timing | Purpose | Subject Pattern |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Immediate | Deliver lead magnet + set expectations | "Your [resource] is ready" |
| 2 | Day 3 | Related insight | "The #1 thing about [topic] most people miss" |
| 3 | Day 6 | Case study | "How [Customer] achieved [result]" |
| 4 | Day 10 | Problem agitation | "Are you still struggling with [pain point]?" |
| 5 | Day 14 | Solution overview | "The [framework] for solving [problem]" |
| 6 | Day 18 | Product intro (soft) | "How [Brand] helps with [problem]" |
| 7 | Day 22 | Social proof | "[X] companies trust [Brand] for [use case]" |
| 8 | Day 26 | Direct offer | "Start your [free trial / consultation]" |
| 9 | Day 30 | Urgency / scarcity | "Last chance: [offer details]" |
| 10 | Day 35 | Long-term value | "Whether or not you're ready, here's [evergreen value]" |

## Re-engagement Sequence

**Trigger:** No opens or clicks in 30-90 days
**Goal:** Win back attention or clean the list
**Duration:** 10-14 days, 3-5 emails

| Email | Timing | Purpose | Subject Pattern |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Day 0 | "We miss you" | "It's been a while, [Name]" |
| 2 | Day 4 | Best content recap | "In case you missed: our top [resources]" |
| 3 | Day 8 | Exclusive offer | "A special [offer] just for you" |
| 4 | Day 12 | Final notice | "Should we keep sending you emails?" |
| 5 | Day 14 | Goodbye (auto-unsub) | "We've paused your emails" |

**Branch at Email 3:** If opens -> move back to main list. If no activity after Email 4 -> auto-unsubscribe to maintain list health.

## Win-back Sequence

**Trigger:** Cancelled subscription or stopped purchasing
**Goal:** Recover churned customers
**Duration:** 14-21 days, 3-4 emails

| Email | Timing | Purpose | Subject Pattern |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Day 0 | Acknowledge + feedback | "We're sorry to see you go" |
| 2 | Day 5 | Show what they're missing | "Here's what's new at [Brand]" |
| 3 | Day 10 | Incentive offer | "Come back: [discount/offer]" |
| 4 | Day 18 | Final outreach | "Door is always open, [Name]" |

## Product Launch Sequence

**Trigger:** Scheduled launch date
**Goal:** Build anticipation, drive launch-day conversions
**Duration:** 2-3 weeks pre-launch + launch week, 5-8 emails

| Email | Timing | Purpose | Subject Pattern |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 14 days before | Teaser | "Something big is coming..." |
| 2 | 10 days before | Problem/need | "The [problem] nobody talks about" |
| 3 | 7 days before | Sneak peek | "First look: [Product Name]" |
| 4 | 3 days before | Early access / waitlist | "Be first to get [Product]" |
| 5 | Launch day | Announcement | "[Product] is here!" |
| 6 | Day after launch | Social proof | "The response has been incredible" |
| 7 | 3 days after | FAQ / objection handling | "Your top questions about [Product], answered" |
| 8 | 7 days after | Last chance (if limited) | "Don't miss out on [offer]" |

## Cart Abandonment Sequence

**Trigger:** Items added to cart but no checkout
**Goal:** Recover abandoned carts
**Duration:** 3 days, 3 emails

| Email | Timing | Purpose | Subject Pattern |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1 hour | Reminder | "You left something behind" |
| 2 | 24 hours | Social proof + FAQ | "Others love [product] — any questions?" |
| 3 | 72 hours | Incentive | "Still thinking? Here's [discount]" |

## Post-Purchase Sequence

**Trigger:** Completed purchase
**Goal:** Reduce churn, increase satisfaction, generate referrals
**Duration:** 30 days, 4-5 emails

| Email | Timing | Purpose | Subject Pattern |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Immediate | Confirmation + next steps | "Your order is confirmed!" |
| 2 | Day 3 | Getting started guide | "Get the most out of [Product]" |
| 3 | Day 10 | Check-in + tips | "How's it going with [Product]?" |
| 4 | Day 20 | Review request | "We'd love your feedback" |
| 5 | Day 30 | Referral / cross-sell | "Share [Brand] with a friend" |
