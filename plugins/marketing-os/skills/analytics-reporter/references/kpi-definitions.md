# Marketing KPI Definitions

Standard KPIs tracked by the Analytics Reporter, organized by category.

## SEO & Organic Search

| KPI | Definition | Source | Good Trend |
|---|---|---|---|
| Organic Traffic | Monthly visitors from organic search | Site Explorer / Web Analytics | Up |
| Organic Keywords | Total keywords ranking in top 100 | Site Explorer | Up |
| Top 3 Keywords | Keywords in positions 1-3 | Site Explorer / GSC | Up |
| Top 10 Keywords | Keywords in positions 1-10 | Site Explorer / GSC | Up |
| Domain Rating (DR) | Ahrefs authority score (0-100) | Site Explorer | Up |
| Referring Domains | Unique domains linking to the site | Site Explorer | Up |
| Avg. Position | Average search position across all keywords | GSC | Down (lower = better) |
| CTR | Click-through rate from search results | GSC | Up |
| Impressions | Times pages appeared in search results | GSC | Up |
| Clicks | Organic clicks from search results | GSC | Up |

## Traffic & Engagement

| KPI | Definition | Source | Good Trend |
|---|---|---|---|
| Total Visitors | Unique visitors in period | Web Analytics | Up |
| Pageviews | Total page views | Web Analytics | Up |
| Bounce Rate | % visitors who leave after one page | Web Analytics | Down |
| Avg. Session Duration | Mean time per visit | Web Analytics | Up |
| Pages per Session | Average pages viewed per visit | Web Analytics | Up |
| Entry Page Conversion | % of landing page visitors who convert | Web Analytics | Up |

## Content Performance

| KPI | Definition | Source | Good Trend |
|---|---|---|---|
| Top Pages (traffic) | Pages driving most organic traffic | Site Explorer | Stable/Up |
| New Pages Indexed | New pages appearing in search | GSC | Up (if quality) |
| Content Velocity | New content published per period | Manual tracking | Depends on strategy |
| Traffic per Page | Average organic traffic per indexed page | Calculated | Up |

## Competitive

| KPI | Definition | Source | Good Trend |
|---|---|---|---|
| Share of Voice | % of total search visibility vs competitors | Rank Tracker / Brand Radar | Up |
| Common Keywords | Keywords overlapping with competitors | Site Explorer | Context-dependent |
| AI Visibility | Brand mentions in AI-generated responses | Brand Radar | Up |
| AI SOV | Share of voice in AI platforms | Brand Radar | Up |

## Reporting Cadences

### Daily (automated check)
- Organic Traffic (vs yesterday, vs same day last week)
- Critical Site Audit issues
- New/lost keywords in top 10

### Weekly
- All SEO & Organic Search KPIs with week-over-week comparison
- Top 10 pages by traffic with changes
- Channel breakdown
- Quick competitor snapshot (DR + traffic)

### Monthly
- All KPIs with month-over-month and year-over-year
- Full competitive analysis
- Content performance deep dive
- Backlink growth analysis
- Device and geographic trends
- Action items for next month

### Quarterly
- Strategic review of all KPIs
- Goal progress tracking
- Market positioning changes
- Content audit recommendations
- Channel ROI analysis
