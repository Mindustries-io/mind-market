# Content Frameworks

Proven copywriting and content frameworks for different marketing objectives.

## AIDA - Attention, Interest, Desire, Action

**Best for:** Landing pages, ads, awareness content, email

| Stage | Purpose | Techniques |
|---|---|---|
| Attention | Stop the scroll | Bold headline, surprising stat, provocative question |
| Interest | Build curiosity | Relevant problem, story, data point |
| Desire | Create want | Benefits (not features), social proof, transformation |
| Action | Drive conversion | Clear CTA, urgency, risk reversal |

## PAS - Problem, Agitate, Solution

**Best for:** Blog posts, email sequences, product pages

| Stage | Purpose | Techniques |
|---|---|---|
| Problem | Identify the pain | Name the specific problem the reader faces |
| Agitate | Amplify urgency | Describe consequences of not solving, emotional impact |
| Solution | Present the answer | Your product/service/advice as the resolution |

## BAB - Before, After, Bridge

**Best for:** Case studies, transformation stories, testimonials

| Stage | Purpose | Techniques |
|---|---|---|
| Before | Current state | Paint the picture of the reader's current situation |
| After | Desired state | Show what life looks like after the transformation |
| Bridge | How to get there | Your solution is the bridge between before and after |

## 4Ps - Promise, Picture, Proof, Push

**Best for:** Product launches, feature announcements, sales pages

| Stage | Purpose | Techniques |
|---|---|---|
| Promise | Bold claim | State the main benefit clearly |
| Picture | Visualization | Help reader imagine having the benefit |
| Proof | Evidence | Data, testimonials, case studies, demos |
| Push | Urgency | Limited time, scarcity, next step |

## QUEST - Qualify, Understand, Educate, Stimulate, Transition

**Best for:** Educational content, guides, thought leadership

| Stage | Purpose | Techniques |
|---|---|---|
| Qualify | Filter audience | "This is for you if..." |
| Understand | Show empathy | Demonstrate you understand their situation |
| Educate | Deliver value | Teach something actionable |
| Stimulate | Inspire action | Show what's possible with this knowledge |
| Transition | Next step | Guide to the next piece of content or CTA |

## Content Type Matrix

| Content Type | Primary Framework | Word Count | Intent |
|---|---|---|---|
| Blog post (how-to) | QUEST | 1500-3000 | Informational |
| Blog post (listicle) | PAS | 1000-2000 | Informational |
| Blog post (comparison) | 4Ps | 2000-3000 | Commercial |
| Landing page | AIDA | 500-1500 | Transactional |
| Case study | BAB | 800-1500 | Commercial |
| Product page | 4Ps | 500-1000 | Transactional |
| Email (nurture) | PAS | 200-500 | Informational |
| Email (sales) | AIDA | 200-400 | Transactional |
| Press release | Inverted pyramid | 400-800 | Informational |
| Social post | Hook + value + CTA | 50-300 | Varies |
