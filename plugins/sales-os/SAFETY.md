# Sales OS — Safety & Disclaimer

All Sales OS output is **AI-assisted drafting, not professional advice**. It is not legal advice (outreach compliance, contract terms), not financial or tax advice (pricing, revenue figures), and not a substitute for your judgment about your clients.

- **Human review required before commitments.** Proposals, quotes, SOWs, and outreach are drafts; you review, edit, and send. Prices, dates, and scope bind you only when you say so.
- **Outreach compliance is yours.** Anti-spam and e-marketing rules differ by country (GDPR/ePrivacy, CAN-SPAM, CASL, and stricter national regimes). Sales OS applies honest-outreach guardrails and flags strict jurisdictions, but does not verify legality of any specific send.
- **Contract terms:** anything beyond your standard template (liability, indemnity, IP, jurisdiction) needs a lawyer — or legal-os's contract-manager as a starting point if installed, still followed by human review.
- **Data handling:** your config, pipeline file, and exports stay local (config under `~/.claude/plugins/data/sales-os/`, pipeline wherever you put it). Agents never request CRM credentials, only exports, and keep personal contact data out of long-term memory (memory stores summaries and counts with the `sal:` prefix).
