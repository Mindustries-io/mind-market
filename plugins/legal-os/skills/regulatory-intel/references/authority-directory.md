## One-Stop-Shop Mechanism

Under GDPR's one-stop-shop (Art. 56), the **lead supervisory authority** is determined by the location of the controller's main establishment (where central administration decisions are made).

**To determine the lead DPA:**
1. Where is the org's main establishment in the EU?
2. Where are decisions about data processing purposes and means made?
3. The DPA of that Member State is the lead authority

**Cross-border cases:** Other "concerned" DPAs can raise objections through the consistency mechanism (Art. 60-66).
