# EU Regulatory Calendar — Key Dates

## GDPR (Regulation (EU) 2016/679)
- **In force since:** May 25, 2018
- **Ongoing:** Continuous compliance required
- **Upcoming:** ePrivacy Regulation (replacement for Cookie Directive) — delayed, no firm date

## NIS2 Directive (Directive (EU) 2022/2555)
- **Published:** December 27, 2022
- **Member State transposition deadline:** October 17, 2024 (many states are late)
- **Applicability:** Essential and important entities across 18 sectors
- **Key obligation dates:**
  - Management body training and oversight: from transposition
  - Incident reporting to CSIRT: from transposition
  - Supply chain security requirements: from transposition
  - Registration with competent authorities: varies by Member State

## AI Act (Regulation (EU) 2024/1689)
- **Published:** August 1, 2024
- **Entry into force:** August 1, 2024
- **Key milestone dates:**
  - **February 2, 2025:** Prohibited AI practices ban takes effect
  - **August 2, 2025:** GPAI model obligations apply; governance structures must be established
  - **August 2, 2026:** Main provisions apply (high-risk AI in Annex III)
  - **August 2, 2027:** High-risk AI systems in Annex I (regulated products) requirements apply

## DORA (Regulation (EU) 2022/2554)
- **Published:** December 27, 2022
- **Application date:** January 17, 2025
- **Applies to:** Financial entities (banks, insurance, investment firms, payment institutions, crypto-asset providers)
- **Key requirements from application:**
  - ICT risk management framework
  - ICT incident reporting
  - Digital operational resilience testing
  - ICT third-party risk management
  - Information sharing arrangements

## CSRD (Directive (EU) 2022/2464)
- **Published:** December 16, 2022
- **Phased application:**
  - **FY 2024 (report in 2025):** Companies already subject to NFRD (large public-interest entities >500 employees)
  - **FY 2025 (report in 2026):** Large companies (2 of 3: >250 employees, >€50M turnover, >€25M assets)
  - **FY 2026 (report in 2027):** Listed SMEs (with opt-out until 2028)
  - **FY 2028 (report in 2029):** Non-EU companies with >€150M EU turnover

## ePrivacy (Current: Directive 2002/58/EC)
- **Current directive:** In force since 2002, amended 2009
- **Proposed ePrivacy Regulation:** Stalled in EU legislative process
- **Key current requirements:**
  - Cookie consent (implementing guidance varies by Member State)
  - Electronic communications confidentiality
  - Direct marketing consent requirements

## Data Act (Regulation (EU) 2023/2854)
- **Published:** December 22, 2023
- **Application date:** September 12, 2025
- **Applies to:** IoT manufacturers, data holders, data recipients, cloud providers
- **Key requirements:**
  - Right of users to access data generated by connected products
  - B2B data sharing obligations
  - Cloud switching rights
  - International data transfer safeguards for non-personal data

## Digital Markets Act (DMA)
- **Application date:** May 2, 2023 (designation: September 2023)
- **Applies to:** Designated gatekeepers (currently 7 companies)
- **Key compliance deadline passed:** March 6, 2024

## Digital Services Act (DSA)
- **Full application:** February 17, 2024
- **Applies to:** Online platforms, hosting services, search engines
- **Key ongoing obligations:** Content moderation, transparency reporting, risk assessments
